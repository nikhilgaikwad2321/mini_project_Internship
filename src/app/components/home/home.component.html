Welcome to EatsExpress

Follow these steps to add your restaurant to our website and start delivering to customers:

Step 1: Register Your Restaurant

Provide all necessary details about your restaurant including name, owner, location, and contact information.

Step 2: Upload Menu and Prices

Add your restaurant's menu along with prices for each item. Ensure that all information is accurate and up-to-date.

Step 3: Set Up Delivery Options

Configure your delivery options, including delivery areas, charges, and timings.

Step 4: Start Receiving Orders

Once your restaurant is set up, start receiving orders from customers and begin delivering delicious food.
